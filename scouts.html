My Experience

I joined just about a year ago in october, but I have already learned a lot of first aid and life skills like what to do when you get lost or how to start a fire anywhere. I have gone a lot of monthly campouts, and all of them have been fun.

My favorite Campouts

One of my favorite campouts was murray lodge because we stayed in a lodge at marin sierra and although it was cold it was cool to see the entire lake frozen over and having snowball fights. I think the best part of the campout I think was the evening we spent playing uno or trying to scare eachother.

Another one of my favorite campouts was actually the week long summer camp also at marin sierra where I learned lifesaving and first aid but also had a lot of fun playing in the unfrozen lake and also I celebrated my birthday there.
